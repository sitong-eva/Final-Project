    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What is tarot reading?

      Tarot reading is a divination practice that uses a deck of tarot cards to gain insight and guidance on different aspects of life.

    • How does tarot reading work?

      During a tarot reading, a tarot reader draws and interprets cards based on the question asked by the client, offering guidance and insight based on the symbolism and meaning of the cards.

    • Is tarot reading accurate?

      Tarot reading is not a science, but a spiritual practice that provides insight and guidance based on interpretation of symbols and archetypes. The accuracy of a reading can vary depending on the skill and intuition of the reader, as well as the client's openness to the reading.

    • How often should I get a tarot reading?

      The frequency of tarot readings depends on personal preference and the type of question being asked. Some people prefer to get a reading once a month, while others may only seek a reading for major life decisions or changes.

    • Can tarot reading predict the future?

      Tarot reading is not meant to predict the future, but rather to offer guidance and insight based on the current energies and potential outcomes of a situation. The future is always subject to change based on free will and external factors.

    • Can tarot readings be done remotely or online?

      Yes, tarot readings can be done remotely or online through various mediums such as video chat, phone call, or messaging. With modern technology, distance is no longer a barrier to receiving a tarot reading, as the cards are drawn and interpreted based on the energy and intention of the reader and client. Remote tarot readings are just as accurate and insightful as in-person readings, and can be a convenient option for those who cannot visit a physical location for a reading.
